Challenge Overview

Participants are part of a group of engineers working for FIRE Rockets which has been contracted to build a rocket to deliver a payload to astronauts on the International Space Station (ISS) and bring one astronaut (an egg) safely back to Earth. The rocket must reach a minimum of 160ft and contain a payload of at least one penny during test flights and one large egg during the final launch.
